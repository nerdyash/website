Life Band Disaster Management Tool-kit : Pre-Research Prototyping


Developed Disaster management tool kit as my final year project of Bachelor Degree.It was pre research prototype using Arduino, GPS, Android device and laptop as hardware. This disaster management tool kit will help rescue team to find the location of people who are trapped in any disaster. The tool kit has three parts – one with the individual, other with the rescue team and third in the server. The device with individuals is called Life Band and anyone in distress situation can simply press a button on Life Band, wherein a distress signal will be relayed via communication device to rescue team, using an Android Device loaded with Maps to identify and rescue the individual.
